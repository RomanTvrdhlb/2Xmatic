how it works?

X2tron.pro is a fully decentralized investment platform built on a smart contract in the TRON network. The smart contract is open and verifiable, it can’t be corrupted or tampered with. Blockchain technology ensures 100% fairness and transparency of the fund.

  • CRYPTO WALLET

    To start investing users must be logged in to a crypto wallet The most popular Tron wallets that have the most users are Tronlink, TronWallet.

  • SMART CONTRACT

    X2tron smart contract generates 20% of the investment amount daily and guarantees a final profit of 200% in 10 days.

  • INVESTMENT PROFIT

    The profit (dividends) accrued to your account on a daily basis and in real-time. You can withdraw the dividends at any time.
